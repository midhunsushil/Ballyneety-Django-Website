A Legacy for Quality Breeding

Our Mares

On the subject of genetics, experts agree that while breeding is not an exact science, it is however a key performance indicator. This is a view taken rather seriously at Ballyneety Stud Farm, particularly because of the demands placed upon the modern sporthorse.

The generations of horses bred here have a rich genetic heritage. The breeding lines of performers past like Orange Peel, Cottage Son, Ladykiller, Hyperion, Dark Ronald & St.Simon are all present, as are Nearco & Bold Ruler (Sire of Secretariat !!). There is a “large splash of the Irish “Holy Trinity” of King of Diamonds, Clover Hill & Highland Flight.

Of the 12- 15 mares bred here annually, all of them posess classic lines, infused over the years with the likes of Sunny light, Horos, Warcraft, Errigal Flight, Beneficial, Carnival Night, Rhett Butler, Imperious, Golden Beaker, High Roller, Sky Boy, Master Imp, Hail Station, Cruising, Dancing Brave, & Ballinvella, amongst others.
