Butlers Cravat

“Butler” is a very special horse, being by Errigal Flight (King of Diamonds x Highland Flight) out of a Rhett Butler mare. He is a very good looking, very athletic horse, & with that pedigree he is passing all that ability & temperament on to his offspring. Errigal flight was the father of Ado Annie amongst others & that King of Diamonds / Highland flight cross sired an absolute dynasty of sporthorses.
The maternal line of Rhett Butler (thoroughbred) is very interesting as it is a rare line today. They were such good performers that they were good performers, few people had the foresight in Ireland to retain a few for breeding purposes. Ballyneety is a virtual treasure trove of the best Irish blood lines & Butlers Cravat is but one of the fine examples of the breeding philosophy here.

MY O MY

Counting among the most versatile sires standing in Ireland, My O My is a well related Holstein stallion by Loutano out of renowned broodmare Bilda by Silvester. He is recognised not only as the sire of numerous successful show jumpers, eventers and show horses, but importantly, is well known for the trainable temperament of his stock. Punching well above his weight in terms of mare numbers to successes, the results of his progeny has been noteworthy and far reaching. Boasting a superb performance pedigree, especially through his maternal line, My O My is out of Bilda (Silvester), who has bred several 1.60m show jumpers including, Casco 2, Calvano and Catoki. A half-brother to My O My, the latter has become a particularly influential sire, and among his many world class offspring is the WEG and European championship mare Catch Me If You Can. This outstanding mare made headlines when changing hands for a record-breaking sum two years ago, and is now owned by the Czech Equestrian Federation. My O My has been well represented on the world stage, and in eventing especially, his limited offspring have regularly shone at international level. Due to his outstanding temperament, he not only suits the professional rider, but appears regularly in the young rider and junior ranks.
